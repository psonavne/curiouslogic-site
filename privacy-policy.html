By using the Site or the App, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information we collect and how we use it

Website

We do not require you to create an account to browse the Site. We may collect limited technical information automatically, such as browser type, general device or connection information, and approximate region derived from standard server and analytics logs. We use this data to operate, secure, and improve the Site, understand aggregate traffic patterns, and comply with applicable law.

If you contact us by email or through links we provide, we process the contents of your message and your contact details only to respond to you and to maintain our records as permitted by law.

Mogul Rising (App)

No user accounts are required to play Mogul Rising. Game progress (including token balances, player statistics, and related gameplay state) is stored locally on your device; we do not require you to register or sign in to our servers for core gameplay. On supported Apple devices, data may additionally sync through your Apple ID via iCloud according to Apple’s privacy terms.

On Android, equivalent on-device storage may apply; cloud backup behavior depends on your device and Google account settings. We design the App to minimize collection of personally identifiable information beyond what is needed for ads (see below) and normal platform services.

2. Advertising and third-party services

For the mobile app Mogul Rising, we use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. On Apple devices, Google AdMob may collect and use your device’s IDFA (Identifier for Advertisers), along with other technical data allowed by Google’s SDK, to deliver and measure ads—including personalized ads when permitted by your settings and applicable law. On Android, similar identifiers (such as the Google Advertising ID) may be used for the same purposes.

Google processes this information under its own policies; see Google’s Privacy Policy and Advertising technologies for how Google uses ad-related data.

Where we publish an app-ads.txt file on the Site, it identifies our authorized digital sellers as required by industry practice.

Your choices. On iPhone and iPad, you can manage tracking and ad-related preferences in Settings > Privacy & Security—including Tracking and Apple Advertising—which controls whether apps may access or use the IDFA for personalized ads in line with Apple’s rules. On Android, you can reset or opt out of interest-based ads in Settings > Google > Ads (wording may vary by device).

3. Data security and retention

We take reasonable steps to protect information we handle and work with reputable platform providers under their security standards. Website logs and correspondence are retained only as long as needed for the purposes described above or as required by law. On-device App data remains under your control unless you delete the App or clear app data, subject to your platform’s backup behavior.

4. Children’s privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). If you believe we have collected personal information from a child without appropriate consent, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

If you access the Site or App from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States or other countries where we or our service providers operate. We may update this policy from time to time; the “Last updated” date below will change when we do. Continued use of the Site or App after changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy where permitted by law.
